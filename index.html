// HOW TO RUN THE GRID

◆ Your Hand

Each turn you draw 4 cards. Click or tap a card to play it. Cards cost energy shown as ◆ pips — you get 3 energy per turn.

⏎ End Your Turn

Click [ END TURN ] when done playing cards. The enemy then attacks. Repeat until one of you hits 0 HP.

🛡 Block

DEFEND gives you block. Block absorbs incoming damage first. It resets to zero at the start of each new turn — use it or lose it.

⬆ Between Rooms

After each fight, pick 1 of 3 cards to add to your deck. Your deck reshuffles automatically when the draw pile runs out.
